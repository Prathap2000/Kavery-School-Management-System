History Of Kaveri Educational Trust :


Our organization, in 1987, has strived to improve the educational level of rural children over the past years by providing a good education for children in rural areas. Dr. Krishnan said that our institute is the best school in the state level in examinations in Chitradurga with 100% result in 7th standard examination all year. Dr. Shivayogi Hiremath, a renowned scientist and Vice Chancellor of India from Kuvempu University Karnataka won the first place in the Taluk Junior Primary School Level Painting Competition for the year 2000-2001. The trust aims to adopt the objectives for all-round development for the children in physical, psychological activities





Objectives Of Kaveri Educational Trust :


1. Beginning on August 15, 1987 with a well-intentioned education for rural students.
2. To provide good elementary education to the children of the country side without any business or profit.
3. We are the only organization in our rural area that has been serving our community from 34 years with great success, honest and unblemished.
4. Based on these 34 years of experience, our goal is to make every student's academic activity better by realizing the mental capacity of each student, planning and teaching accordingly.
5. Furnished building, covered with furnishings furniture.
6. Prefer teaching two languages, Kannada and English. (Only English For 6th And 7th)
7. As a result of the service rendered by the Institute, 90% of all our students are working in various posts in the main memorials of the society of the students, including Doctor, Engineer, Lecturer in various departments of the State and Central Government.
8. Our trust have been asked to enroll their children in our educational institute for a good education with very low fees and for the bright future of their children.
